My Services

IEEE

I am an active IEEE member with involvement in multiple societies, including SPS, TEMS, IAS, WIE, COMSOC, PELS, and PES. Currently, I serve as the IEEE TEMS Chairperson at my college and as the IEEE SPS Kerala Section Newsletter Editor. I have had the opportunity to host the AGM 2025 and participate in various IEEE workshops, including PCB designing, Python programming, emerging technologies in green energy, thermin workshops, and 6G and beyond. Through these experiences, I’ve enhanced my technical skills while actively contributing to the IEEE community.

These roles have further developed my leadership, organizational, and technical skills.
